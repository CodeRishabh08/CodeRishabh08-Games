How to Play - Circle Defence



Defend Yourself from Enemies all Around.



Controls



Use Your Mouse To Shoot at Your Enemies.



Enemies



  • Those Circular Bodies Which will be coming towards you are enemies.
  • They will have different health and colour.
  • some of them will need more than one bullet to die.
  • if they touch you, your health will be affected according to the size of the enemy



Enjoy



Make and Break High Scores, Explore more while Playing the game
