How to play - Get Shoot Run



Your Goal is to steal the white - blue diamond without getting caught by the Enemies.



Controls



Use WASD or the arrow keys to change the direction of the moving Player Sprite. (the color of the player keeps changing)


Left Click (Mouse) to shoot in the direction of the pointer/crosshair



Diamond



  • You will always see a tiny white-blue coloured stone on canvas, its the DIAMOND
  • Your aim is to steal it, it will appear somewhere else as soon as you touch it
  • Your ammo number will increase but it will also spawn enemies



Enemies



  • Don't let the Enemies touch you, use your mouse to shoot at them
  • They will spawn from inside of the yellow Boxes as soon as you touch the Diamond
  • the number of spawning enemies will increase over time
  • There are 2 types of enemies, red ones and orange ones
  • Red ones will try to catch you but the orange ones are blind, they will just follow your access.
  • These Enemies will Mix up with each other to confuse you, but you can see the original count of enemies in the top right corner of the screen



Bullets



  • Shoot Bullets at Enemies to kill Them
  • You will have limited number of Bullets
  • The number will double when you will steal a diamond
  • you can have maximum 200 bullets with you



Enjoy
